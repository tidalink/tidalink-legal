Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tidalink. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways depending on your interactions with the app, including:

  • Personal Data: Your username, country which you have selected while creating an account, email address, and optionally your profile picture and bio.
  • App Activity: Wishlist items, logged sightings, earned badges, and chat participation.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and timestamps of usage.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Customize your experience and track your progress.
  • Allow communication between users (chat and posts).
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • Send important updates or responses to feedback.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information in the following situations:

  • Compliance with Laws: If required by law, legal process, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of others.
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (e.g., Firebase, analytics tools), under strict data protection terms.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including Firebase Authentication and Firestore access rules. However, no online system is fully secure. Users should also protect their login credentials.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You can update your profile data and theme settings within the app. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Tidalink is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at support@tidalinkapp.com.
